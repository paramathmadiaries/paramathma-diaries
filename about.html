Meet The Founders

Neeraj Jayanna

Founder • Vision & Growth

Neeraj envisioned PARAMATHMA DIARIES as more than a travel company — a movement that connects people emotionally through nature, adventure and unforgettable experiences.

His vision is to build one of Karnataka's most loved travel communities where journeys heal people, inspire growth and create lifelong memories.

Focused on expansion, branding and community culture, he believes travel should transform emotions, not just locations.

Arun

Founder • Experience & Execution

Arun believes the best journeys are created through meaningful experiences, perfect execution and unforgettable moments.

His vision is to make every PARAMATHMA journey feel premium, emotional and deeply memorable through seamless operations and adventure experiences.

Focused on trust, safety and high-quality travel experiences, he aims to redefine how people experience travel communities in Karnataka.
